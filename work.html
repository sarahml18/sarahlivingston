Flair Voyage — Inflight Magazine

Creating an inflight magazine that felt elevated, modern, and brand-aligned — while engaging a captive audience and opening the door to future advertising revenue. The kind of inflight magazine you actually want to read.

The challenge

Flair needed a publication that balanced inspiration, utility, and brand storytelling — without feeling like traditional airline marketing. The magazine had to work hard: delight passengers mid-flight and serve as a scalable revenue vehicle through advertising partnerships.

What I did

  • Wrote and edited all editorial copy across multiple issues
  • Developed bilingual EN/FR content for Quebec-facing spreads
  • Created destination features, PTO strategy content, and travel quizzes
  • Managed stakeholder review and approval workflows
  • Built a scalable content framework for future issues and seatback ads

Advertorial pieces opened the door to authentic tourism board and travel trade partnerships, increasing revenue while elevating the brand. A scalable content framework is now in place for future opportunities including seatback ads and digital content.

Customer Experience 2024 Copywriter · Editor · Project Manager · Stakeholder Alignment

Disruption Messages — Customer Communications

Rewriting Flair's flight disruption notifications to be more transparent, human, and clear — without overpromising or creating operational risk. 300+ templates, all rethought from the ground up.

The challenge

When flights are delayed or cancelled, passengers are already stressed. The existing templates leaned on vague, legal-safe language that prioritized brevity over clarity — leaving customers feeling anxious and in the dark. The brief was to fix that, without creating new problems for the operations team.

What I did

  • Audited and rewrote 300+ SMS and email disruption notification templates
  • Developed a consistent, human brand voice for high-stress moments
  • Balanced transparency with operational accuracy at every touchpoint
  • Managed multi-stakeholder review across ops, legal, and customer experience teams
  • Built a scalable template system for future communications

Every disruption message now leads with empathy, explains the situation clearly, and gives passengers the specific information they need — without hedging language that erodes trust at the worst possible moment.
